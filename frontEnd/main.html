Achieving the Performance Goal: Latency Reduction

Current Target

3 - 5 SECONDS

Reported latency for end-to-end transcription and translation.

Optimization Methods

  • **Early Sentence Segmentation:** Translate segments, not whole sentences.
  • **Smaller NMT Models:** Use efficient, optimized models over large, slow ones.
  • **Caching:** Reuse common phrases/terms to avoid repeated processing.
  • **Optimized Beam Search:** Faster decoding in the translation process.

Evolution of Machine Translation: From Rules to AI

1Ô∏è‚É£

Rule-Based MT (RBMT)

**Era:** Pre-2000s
**Core:** Strict, hand-coded linguistic rules and dictionaries.
**Drawback:** Lacks fluency and fails entirely on ambiguity or new slang.

2Ô∏è‚É£

Statistical MT (SMT)

**Era:** 2000s - Early 2010s
**Core:** Learns translation probabilities from massive bilingual text corpora.
**Drawback:** Word-based approach often ignores global sentence context, leading to unnatural output.

3Ô∏è‚É£

Neural MT (NMT)

**Era:** Mid-2010s - Present
**Core:** Uses transformer architecture (Deep Learning) to process the entire sentence context.
**Advantage:** Achieves human-like fluency, superior accuracy, and handles syntax/idioms well.
